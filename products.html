Software development

ContextHub

One living context layer for every coding agent.

Web + CLIAgent-nativeLocal analysis

AI coding agents can write code quickly, but every tool begins with an incomplete understanding of the product, architecture and decisions behind the repository.

ContextHub analyzes a repository locally, identifies important gaps, turns uncertainty into ranked questions, and maintains canonical project context that can be rendered for multiple coding agents.

The everyday problem is repeated explanation and context drift. ContextHub lets a team answer once and keep the understanding current.

Data analysis

EDP Assist

A governed AI analyst that builds the answer live.

30 agents38 skillsRead-only

Business and data questions often begin in plain language but end in an opaque answer, a long analyst queue, or a notebook only one person understands.

EDP Assist turns the question into a bounded plan, shows the plan before complex work begins, and builds the analysis visibly inside Jupyter. The work is narrated as it runs and the final answer cites the executed cells that support it.

The everyday problem is getting from a question to analysis you can inspect. EDP Assist keeps the reasoning, method and evidence attached to the answer.

Personal health

EaseMyDisease

Your health information, ready before you need it.

Health vaultCare accessEmergency

Personal health information is usually spread across reports, prescriptions, providers, family messages and memory. The fragmentation becomes most painful precisely when information needs to move quickly.

EaseMyDisease brings records, medication context, family profiles, body-linked health information, care access and emergency context into one user-controlled experience.

The everyday problem is continuity. A person's health history is continuous; the product is designed to make their information continuous too.

Food & nutrition

MyFoodCraving

Personalized nutrition that ends in a cooked meal.

RecommendationsGuided cookingLocal chefs

Nutrition products often stop after telling people what they should eat. Recipe products assume the user already knows what fits. Chef marketplaces start only after the food decision has been made.

MyFoodCraving connects personalized meal recommendations, plain-language reasons, dietary constraints, recipe guidance, meal tracking and local chef discovery in one practical food loop.

The everyday problem is not finding information about food. It is getting from personal needs to a meal that actually gets made.
