01 / AI context infrastructure

ContextHub

One living context layer for every coding agent.

ContextWeb + CLIAgent-native

AI coding tools are becoming part of the engineering team. Their project context is not.

ContextHub analyzes a repository locally, identifies uncertain or incomplete assumptions, turns the gaps into ranked questions, and maintains one canonical understanding of product, architecture, requirements and plans.

Answer once. Strengthen the context. Synchronize it across agents.

02 / Agentic analytics

EDP Assist

A governed AI analyst that builds the answer live.

30 agents38 skillsRead-only

Ask a data question in plain English. EDP Assist rates the complexity, composes a bounded plan, and shows the plan before complex work begins.

Then the analysis builds live inside Jupyter. Every section explains what is being measured, why it matters, how it is being calculated, and what the finding means.

The final answer cites the executed analysis that supports it.

04 / Personalized nutrition

MyFoodCraving

Personalized nutrition that ends in a cooked meal.

RecommendationsGuided cookingLocal chefs

Most nutrition products stop at advice. MyFoodCraving continues until there is food on the table.

The recommendation engine balances hard dietary eligibility, energy, nutrition priorities, optional measured health markers, preferences, variety and everyday effort. Every recommendation explains why it fits.

Choose a meal, cook it step by step, or move from the recipe to a local chef.
