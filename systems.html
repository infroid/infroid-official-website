Systems

Products should stand alone.
Portfolios should compound.

Infroid's four products remain independent by default. The portfolio becomes more powerful when one product can make a narrow, explicit handoff to another without collapsing them into a shared database or one giant app.

DISJOINT BY DEFAULTSeparate users, stores, permissions and product promises. COMPOSE BY CONSENTOnly scoped context crosses a boundary, for one named purpose.
Six pairwise systems

Every permutation has a job.

These are portfolio compositions: what each pair can unlock through a controlled handoff. They are not a requirement for either product to be useful alone.

ContextHub×EDP Assist
01 / THE EVIDENCE LOOP

Turn product assumptions into questions the data can answer.

ContextHub identifies an uncertain, high-impact assumption in project context. The exact question and approved business definitions move to EDP Assist. EDP builds the analysis, returns a cited Answer card, and ContextHub attaches the evidence to the assumption or plan.

Assumption→Analysis→Evidence→Context→Plan
Coding agents know what the team believes. The portfolio can also show what the data supports.
EaseMyDisease×MyFoodCraving
02 / THE HEALTH-TO-FOOD LOOP

Let selected health context shape food, then carry the recommendation into the kitchen.

A person chooses eligible wellness signals from EaseMyDisease to share. MyFoodCraving uses only those selected inputs to reprioritize nutrition goals, explains why a meal fits, and continues into guided cooking or a chef request.

Health context→Nutrition priority→Meal reason→Cook
The loop does not stop at a health record or a recommendation. It reaches the meal.
EaseMyDisease×EDP Assist
03 / PERSONAL HEALTH ANALYTICS

Ask longitudinal questions across your own exported health history and inspect the work.

EaseMyDisease can assemble a user-selected, structured export of records, medication adherence, laboratory history and other personal health events. EDP Assist can analyze that dataset read-only, plan the work visibly, and produce a cited notebook.

Selected export→Read-only plan→Notebook→Cited patterns
Personal analytics without pretending the model is a doctor: inspect patterns, provenance and limits, then take the evidence to a qualified professional.
MyFoodCraving×EDP Assist
04 / THE NUTRITION EXPERIMENT LOOP

Move from meal logging to inspectable personal pattern analysis.

MyFoodCraving contributes meal logs, recommendation reasons, cooking sessions and optional wellness-marker history selected by the user. EDP Assist asks analytical questions such as whether adherence, protein coverage, meal timing or recipe effort changed across periods.

Meals→Question→Analysis→Finding→Next question
The food app explains today's recommendation. The analyst can examine what happened across months.
ContextHub×EaseMyDisease
05 / THE CARE CONTEXT PROTOCOL

Apply canonical-context mechanics to the handoff problem in care.

ContextHub's core idea is that important context should have one canonical form, explicit uncertainty, versions and regenerable projections. EaseMyDisease has the raw ingredients for a user-controlled Care Handoff. The pair suggests a health-context protocol: selected facts, active medications, recent events, open questions and source links packaged as a versioned capsule.

Records→Canonical summary→Open questions→Care handoff
A vault stores information. A context protocol makes the information travel without losing what is uncertain or where it came from.
ContextHub×MyFoodCraving
06 / THE FOOD KNOWLEDGE CONTEXT

Give food and cooking agents a durable understanding of the person, kitchen and constraints.

MyFoodCraving knows dietary eligibility, preferences, saved recipes, recent meals and the practical cooking surface. ContextHub's context model suggests a household food capsule that separates hard constraints from preferences, captures kitchen equipment and recurring answers, and can render a compact context for cooking or shopping agents.

Diet boundaries→Kitchen context→Agent-ready capsule→Meal action
The agent should not rediscover every allergy, disliked cuisine, available utensil and household rule in every conversation.
The 10× product

The missing layer is not another vertical.

It is a trusted way for context and evidence to move between products without creating one omniscient Infroid account.

PROPOSED PRODUCT / INFROID RELAY

Scoped context exchange with provenance, consent and expiry.

Infroid Relay would be the portfolio's connective tissue. Products publish typed, purpose-limited packages instead of opening their databases to one another.

A package can contain a small set of facts, questions, evidence references or analytical outputs. The sender defines the purpose. The user or organization approves the handoff. The receiver sees provenance and expiry. Revocation is first-class.

Why it matters

Four products become a network without becoming a data lake.

Without Relay, every integration is a custom one-off. With Relay, every product speaks the same two primitives: Context Capsule and Evidence Card.

That makes the strongest product combinations repeatable while preserving the disjoint architecture you asked for.

"Share the minimum context needed for one purpose — and make its origin visible."
The 100× product thesis

A personal observatory for longitudinal questions.

This is the product that becomes possible once EMD, MFC, EDP Assist and Relay can work as a governed system.

PROPOSED PRODUCT / INFROID OBSERVATORY

Private, read-only analysis across the personal data you explicitly assemble.

Observatory would let a person create a scoped personal dataset from EaseMyDisease and MyFoodCraving, then ask longitudinal questions through an EDP Assist-derived workbench.

It would not diagnose, prescribe, or silently recommend treatment. Its job is narrower: plan analyses, show methods, cite the underlying events, name limitations, and produce a reviewable report the person can discuss with a qualified professional.

Example questions: How did meal regularity change before and after this period? Which nutrition goals remained consistently unmet? Did medication adherence and logged routine move together? Where are the data gaps too large to support a conclusion?
