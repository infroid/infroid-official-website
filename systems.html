Systems

The portfolio is a
selection engine.

Infroid does not choose products because they fit one industry. We choose problems worth solving comprehensively and prefer products that add reusable capabilities to the systems we already have.

Compounding by selection

Every product should make the next good product easier to build.

The gain may be technical, analytical, operational or domain-specific. What matters is that the portfolio accumulates useful capability rather than unrelated logos.

CONTEXT CAPABILITY

Know more without re-explaining everything.

ContextHub contributes methods for discovering gaps, maintaining current knowledge and preparing agent-ready context. Those patterns can improve how Infroid itself builds and maintains products.

ANALYTICAL CAPABILITY

Ask a question and keep the work visible.

EDP Assist contributes governed planning, read-only analysis, narration and citation. That analytical discipline can support future products wherever longitudinal or operational questions matter.

HEALTH CAPABILITY

Carry user-controlled health information over time.

EaseMyDisease contributes structured personal health continuity, care handoffs and user-controlled sharing patterns for one of the most fragmented everyday domains.

ACTION CAPABILITY

Turn a recommendation into something that happens.

MyFoodCraving contributes explainable personalization, hard constraints, guided execution, routine tracking and a path from a digital recommendation to a real meal.

No parent product

Shared capability does not mean shared identity.

ContextHub is an important foundational capability inside Infroid and can help keep product knowledge, architecture and agent context current across the portfolio. It is also a standalone product with its own customers and value proposition.

The same rule applies to the rest of the portfolio. EDP Assist contributes a governed analysis model. EaseMyDisease contributes longitudinal health continuity. MyFoodCraving contributes a recommendation-to-execution loop.

No product is the company metaphor. Infroid is the system that chooses, builds and compounds them.

“Products stay themselves. Capabilities compound.”
Portfolio combinations

What becomes possible when capabilities meet.

These are system opportunities, not product dependencies. Each combination should preserve clear purpose, user control and the independent value of both products.

ContextHub×EDP Assist
PRODUCT DECISION EVIDENCE

Move from product assumptions to inspectable data questions.

Project context can surface an unresolved assumption. Governed analysis can examine the available data and return a cited finding that product teams can carry back into requirements and planning.

Assumption→Question→Analysis→Decision
Better product context gains evidence; analytics gains the exact business question.
EaseMyDisease×MyFoodCraving
HEALTH-TO-FOOD LOOP

Let selected health context improve everyday food decisions.

A person can choose eligible wellness information to share. MyFoodCraving can use only those selected signals to reprioritize nutrition goals, explain why meals fit, and continue into cooking or a chef request.

Health→Nutrition priority→Meal→Cook
The health record becomes relevant to daily life without exposing the entire health vault.
EaseMyDisease×EDP Assist
PERSONAL HEALTH ANALYTICS

Examine long-term patterns across user-selected health history.

EaseMyDisease can assemble a structured export of selected records, medication adherence, laboratory history and health events. EDP Assist-style analysis can examine the dataset read-only and produce visible, cited work.

Selected history→Plan→Analyze→Review
The user can inspect patterns and data gaps without positioning the analysis as diagnosis or treatment.
MyFoodCraving×EDP Assist
NUTRITION PATTERN ANALYSIS

Go beyond today's meal and examine what happened across months.

Meal logs, recommendation reasons, cooking sessions and selected wellness history can support questions about routine, adherence, meal timing, nutrition coverage and practical recipe effort.

Meals→Question→Analysis→Next question
Personalization handles the day; governed analytics can help examine the pattern over time.
ContextHub×EaseMyDisease
CARE HANDOFF QUALITY

Apply current-context and explicit-uncertainty patterns to health handoffs.

A user-controlled care summary can separate confirmed facts, active medications, recent events, open questions and source records. Context methods help make the handoff current and explicit about what is still unknown.

Records→Current summary→Open questions→Handoff
Health information becomes easier to carry without hiding uncertainty or losing the source trail.
ContextHub×MyFoodCraving
HOUSEHOLD FOOD CONTEXT

Stop rediscovering the same dietary and kitchen constraints.

Hard dietary boundaries, preferences, disliked cuisines, household rules and available utensils can be kept as durable context for cooking and shopping experiences instead of being re-entered in every interaction.

Constraints→Kitchen→Preference→Food action
Food agents and interfaces start with a better understanding of the household they are helping.
The product filter

What should Infroid build next?

A new product should pass both the everyday-problem test and the compounding-effect test.

01 / PROBLEM

Is the problem lived repeatedly?

We favor problems people encounter in normal work, health, food or daily life rather than one-time novelty.

02 / DEPTH

Can we solve more than the visible symptom?

The opportunity should support a comprehensive product loop, not only a thin interface around one model call or workflow step.

03 / LEVERAGE

Do modern tools change what is possible?

AI, data systems, realtime infrastructure or new interfaces should create material product leverage rather than decorative technology.

04 / COMPOUNDING

What capability remains after we build it?

The strongest opportunities add reusable technology, knowledge or operating patterns that expand the next set of problems Infroid can solve.

The systems thesis

“A portfolio compounds when every product solves a real problem today and leaves the company more capable tomorrow. That is the selection discipline.”
