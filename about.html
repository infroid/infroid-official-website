The thesis

Few products.
Considered, deeply.

Most software companies sell velocity. We are trying to sell something rarer: the patience to be right.

Three products, on purpose. Each one earned a long stretch of work before we wrote line one. We are not trying to be everywhere. We are trying to be the right answer in the few places we show up.

"If you cannot say in two sentences why a product has to exist, it does not have to exist."

We are independent and intend to stay that way. The next thing arrives when it is ready, not when a roadmap says it should.

What we do not bend on

Three values.

01 / TASTE

Taste is not optional.

We care about the moment before something becomes measurable: whether it feels clear, useful, and inevitable.

02 / RIGOR

Defend every decision.

We write down why we chose what we chose. Then we revisit it when the product teaches us something better.

03 / KIND

Hard work, calm team.

The problems are hard enough. The way we treat each other through them should make the work easier to hold.

The team

A handful of people.
Generalists with one obsession.

We hire generalists. Most of us write code. All of us write docs. The whole team reviews product specs, regardless of role. If you want to work with us, write to hello@infroid.in with your most considered piece of work.
