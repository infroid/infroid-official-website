Our purpose

Solve more of the real problem.

Many products solve the most visible fragment of a problem: the dashboard, the recommendation, the generated answer, the stored record. Everyday life continues after that fragment.

Infroid exists to build further. We look for the complete practical loop around a problem and use technology to reduce the distance between the user's need and the outcome they were trying to reach.

We choose new products for two reasons: the problem deserves a comprehensive product, and solving it adds capability to the Infroid system.

“Standalone value today. Compounding capability tomorrow.”
How we build

One product doctrine.

The domains can be different. The standard for choosing and building a product stays consistent.

01 / EVERYDAY PROBLEM

Start with something people repeatedly live with.

We favor persistent practical problems over fashionable markets. Repeated friction is a stronger product signal than novelty.

02 / COMPREHENSIVE LOOP

Build past the attractive fragment.

A recommendation should reach action. An answer should retain its evidence. Information should be usable at the moment it matters.

03 / MODERN LEVERAGE

Use the strongest tools available.

AI agents, governed analytics, local processing, realtime systems and recommendation engines are valuable when they materially improve the product outcome.

04 / COMPOUNDING EFFECT

Leave Infroid more capable after every product.

The products remain distinct. The capabilities gained from building them should expand the next set of problems the company can solve well.

The portfolio

Products stay unique. Capabilities travel.

ContextHub solves project-context drift for teams using coding agents. It also gives Infroid strong context and gap-finding patterns for building and maintaining software.

EDP Assist solves the path from a plain-language data question to visible, cited analysis. Its governed analysis model can support products that need reviewable longitudinal questions.

EaseMyDisease solves continuity around personal health information and care moments. MyFoodCraving solves the distance between personalized nutrition and a meal that actually gets made.

None of these products exists to explain the others. Their value propositions remain their own. The compounding effect is a criterion for why Infroid chooses to invest deeply in them.

Stewardship

The company should remain able to follow the product doctrine.

Infroid is being structured around steward-ownership and perpetual-purpose principles: long-term independence, mission-aligned control and purpose-oriented capital.

01 / SELF-DETERMINATION

Control follows responsibility.

Control should remain with people closely connected to Infroid's work and purpose. Governance is a responsibility, not an asset to speculate on.

02 / PURPOSE

Profit funds continuity.

Revenue sustains products, infrastructure, research and the team. Surplus should strengthen the company or advance work aligned with its purpose.

03 / INDEPENDENCE

The mission is not for sale.

We are working toward a binding legal structure that protects Infroid's purpose from a sale, control transfer or governance change that conflicts with it.

Governance status: these principles are Infroid's operating direction. The jurisdiction-specific legal structure is still being formalised, so Infroid does not currently claim to be constituted as a Perpetual Purpose Trust.

Long-horizon company

Designed to keep solving.

PRODUCTS

Depth over product theatre.

We prefer a complete useful system over a thin layer designed mainly to look current.

DATA

Minimum access. Explicit purpose.

We prefer scoped access, read-only analysis, provenance and user-controlled sharing over collecting everything for undefined future use.

CAPITAL

Aligned capital only.

Growth should not require a future sale or a business model that conflicts with a product's purpose. Capital must preserve operating independence.

TIME

Build for the maintenance decade.

We care about the system after launch: understandable architecture, durable operations and the ability to keep improving the product.
