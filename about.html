About Infroid

Built for the work.
Structured to outlast us.

Perpetual purpose

The company is a vehicle for the work.

Infroid exists to build software that increases human agency by turning complex context into clear, inspectable action.

Products may earn revenue, grow and become durable businesses. Profit matters because continuity matters. But profit is a means to sustain the mission, the products and the people doing the work — not a mandate to maximize extraction.

We do not want product priorities, user trust or company control to become negotiating chips in a future exit.

“The purpose should survive the founder, the funding cycle and the market mood.”
Stewardship

Control follows responsibility.

Infroid is being structured around steward-ownership and perpetual-purpose principles: long-term independence, mission-aligned control and purpose-oriented capital.

01 / SELF-DETERMINATION

Stewards hold the wheel.

Control should remain with people closely connected to Infroid's work and purpose. Governance is a responsibility, not an asset to speculate on.

02 / PURPOSE

Profit funds continuity.

Revenue sustains products, infrastructure, research and the team. Surplus should strengthen the company or advance work aligned with its purpose.

03 / INDEPENDENCE

The mission is not for sale.

We are working toward a binding legal structure that protects Infroid's purpose and makes mission reversal or a conventional sell-out structurally difficult.

Legal direction

Principle now. Binding structure next.

Steward-ownership is defined by durable self-determination and purpose-oriented profit. A Perpetual Purpose Trust is one legal mechanism for protecting those commitments; foundation and veto-share structures are other established approaches.

Infroid has adopted these principles as its operating direction. The formal legal implementation is still to be completed for the jurisdiction in which Infroid operates.

Until that work is complete, we will not describe Infroid as already constituted as a Perpetual Purpose Trust. The commitment is to make the governance match the principle — not to borrow the label early.

How we operate

A company designed for long horizons.

01 / PRODUCTS

Independent products first.

Every Infroid product must solve a real problem on its own. Portfolio composition is an advantage, never a requirement for usefulness.

02 / DATA

Minimum context, explicit purpose.

We prefer scoped access, read-only analysis, provenance and user-controlled sharing over collecting everything because it may be useful later.

03 / CAPITAL

Aligned capital only.

Growth should not require a future sale or a business model that conflicts with the product's purpose. Funding must preserve operating independence.

04 / TIME

Build for the maintenance decade.

We care about the system after launch: understandable architecture, explicit constraints, durable operations and the ability to keep improving the work.

The arc

The story so far.

2018
Infroid is founded in India. Early work begins in healthcare software and regional technology problems.
2020
Health becomes a long-running product thesis. The groundwork behind EaseMyDisease deepens.
2024
The portfolio expands around context and action. Agent context and personalized nutrition sharpen into independent systems.
2025
Product architectures harden. ContextHub and MyFoodCraving grow beyond isolated interfaces into durable product systems.
2026
The portfolio reaches four products. EDP Assist adds governed analytics, and Infroid commits its company direction to stewardship, independence and perpetual purpose.
