The problem

A person's health history is continuous. Their health software usually is not.

Reports live in folders. Prescriptions live in messages. Medication context lives in memory. Each provider sees a fragment, and the person is left carrying the context between them.

EaseMyDisease is built around continuity: organize the health context a person chooses to keep, make it usable in everyday care, and surface the relevant pieces when the moment becomes urgent.

Product bridge

Health context can become better food context.

EaseMyDisease + MyFoodCraving

With explicit user consent, eligible wellness markers can travel as a scoped context capsule to MyFoodCraving. MFC can prioritize nutrition goals and explain why a meal fits without receiving the user's entire health vault.

The health-to-food loop stays purposeful, minimal and revocable.
