The problem

A person's health history is continuous. Their health software usually is not.

Reports live in folders. Prescriptions live in messages. Medication information lives in memory. Each provider sees a fragment, and the person is left carrying the information between them.

EaseMyDisease is built around continuity: organize the health information a person chooses to keep, make it usable in everyday care, and surface the relevant pieces when the moment becomes urgent.

Product bridge

Health information can improve everyday food decisions.

EaseMyDisease + MyFoodCraving

With explicit user consent, eligible wellness signals can be shared with MyFoodCraving for one clear purpose. MFC can use those selected signals to reprioritize nutrition goals and explain why a meal fits without receiving the user's entire health vault.

The bridge is selective, purposeful and revocable.
